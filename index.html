The Proposal

Here’s how it happened…

Richie planned a surprise date a week ahead and Mattie was sure excited. He picked her up promptly at 4:45 and they left by 5pm all dressed up. He drove her to a park they have been to many times before to watch the sunset on the river, swing on the bench swing, and have many long conversations. When they walked up to the gazebo she was surprised by a candlelit dinner on the river! A lot of awkward small talked followed as Mattie wondered what was really happening.

Then the moment came…

Not long into dinner (because of his nerves and the ring burning in his pocket) he began to share with her how he loved her and wanted to always love and take care of her. Then Richie got on his knees and asked her to marry him! With a flood of joyful tears, laughing, and shouting, she quickly said yes!!!

Soon after, Richie brought his new fiance over to a surprise engagement party where her parents, his parents, and a lot of sweet friends were all waiting. It was an awesome party and a sweet time with good friends and family. Mattie would say that this was the best surprise she’s ever had, and she wouldn’t have their story any other way.
